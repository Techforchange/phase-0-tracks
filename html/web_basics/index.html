The Road Not Taken

By: Robert Frost

You took the road less traveled...Poem

How I took the road less traveled?

  • Went to college
  • 1st in family to move to another state
  • First to travel around the world alone

Why did I take the road less traveled?

  1. Needed to be a role model for my family
  2. Because my family came to this country for a better future
  3. Because my family supports me in whatever I set my mind to!
